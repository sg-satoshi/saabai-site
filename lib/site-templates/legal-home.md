A bold, human-centered Australian law firm homepage — light and clean with deep navy and rich teal, structured to convert.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Desktop-first (mobile responsive)
- Theme: Light-first with dark accent bands; modern, professional, approachable
- Background: Pure White (#FFFFFF) for nav and content sections
- Surface: Cool Light Gray (#F4F7F9) for alternating sections and card backgrounds
- Primary Accent: Rich Teal (#2AA896) for CTA buttons, icons, numbers, eyebrow labels, check marks, and links
- Dark Band: Deep Navy (#1C2B3A) for stats band and footer
- Text Primary: Deep Navy (#1C2B3A) for all headings
- Text Body: Slate Gray (#4A5568) for body copy and descriptions
- Text On Dark: White (#FFFFFF) for text on navy backgrounds
- Teal On Dark: Rich Teal (#2AA896) for accent numbers and labels on navy background
- Typography: Inter throughout — weight 800 for display headlines, 700 for section headings, 400/500 for body
- Buttons Primary: Rich Teal (#2AA896) fill, white text, 8px radius, medium padding
- Buttons Secondary: White fill with 2px white border, white text — used on dark/photo backgrounds
- Cards: White (#FFFFFF) background, 12px radius, soft shadow (0 2px 12px rgba(0,0,0,0.06)), generous padding
- Eyebrow Labels: Pill-shaped, teal border (#2AA896), teal uppercase text (11px, 0.1em letter-spacing) — used above every section heading
- Icons: Teal (#2AA896) inside a very light teal circle background (#E8F6F4)
- Avatar Circles: Teal (#2AA896) fill, white initials, for testimonial authors without photos
