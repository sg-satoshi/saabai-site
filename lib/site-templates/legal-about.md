The About page for Prestige Legal — warm, credible, and human. Tells the firm's story, establishes values, introduces the team, and showcases credentials.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Desktop-first (mobile responsive)
- Theme: Light-first with dark accent bands — same system as Home and Practice Areas
- Background: Cool Off-White (#F7FAFC) as base
- Surface: Pure White (#FFFFFF) for nav and cards
- Alternating sections: Teal Mist (#E9F7F2) or Frost Gray (#F1F4F6)
- Primary Accent: Forest Teal (#006B5E) for buttons, icons, eyebrow labels, links, teal underlines
- Dark Bands: Deep Charcoal Navy (#0D1D2B) for page hero, stats band, footer
- Icon Circles: Pale Teal (#E4F1EC) background, Forest Teal icon, 48px circle
- Text Headings: Deep Charcoal Navy (#0D1D2B)
- Text Body: Dark Teal-Gray (#3D4946)
- Mid Aqua (#66D9C6) for accent text on dark backgrounds
- Mist Gray (#BCC9C5) for footer text and card borders
- Typography: Inter only — 800 display, 700 headings, 400/500 body
- Cards: White, 12px radius, soft shadow (0 2px 12px rgba(0,0,0,0.06))
- Eyebrow Pills: Forest Teal border + text, pill shape, uppercase 12px — above every heading
- Buttons in content: Forest Teal fill, 8px radius. Buttons in CTA band: pill-shaped (rounded-full)
- Max width: 1200px, 48px horizontal padding
