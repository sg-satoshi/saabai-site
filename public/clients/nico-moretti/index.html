Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly does a companionship engagement include? +

An engagement is a fully tailored social experience. Nico accompanies you to your chosen occasion — whether a corporate function, gala, or private dinner — as a polished, attentive, and intellectually engaging partner. All arrangements are handled with complete professionalism and discretion from first contact to conclusion.

How is my privacy protected? +

Discretion is the foundation of every engagement. Your name, contact details, and the nature of our arrangement are never disclosed to any third party. All correspondence is confidential, invoices are issued discreetly, and Nico maintains absolute professional silence regarding any client at all times.

How do I arrange an engagement? +

Begin by submitting an inquiry through the contact form below. Nico's office will respond within 24 hours to arrange a brief private consultation — by phone or secure message — to discuss the occasion, your expectations, and any specific requirements. From there, all logistics are coordinated seamlessly on your behalf.

How far in advance should I book? +

A minimum of 72 hours notice is appreciated for most engagements, allowing time for proper preparation. For international travel, multi-day commitments, or events requiring a specific wardrobe brief, two weeks or more is preferred. Last-minute requests are considered on a case-by-case basis — do not hesitate to reach out.

Do you travel for engagements? +

Yes. Nico is available for domestic and international engagements. Travel arrangements, accommodation, and associated logistics are coordinated as part of the consultation process. There is no destination too far for the right occasion — Nico is as comfortable at a Milanese gala as a Sydney boardroom dinner.

What topics can Nico speak to in social settings? +

Nico is well-versed across global finance, geopolitics, contemporary art, philanthropy, architecture, and fine dining. He is equally adept at navigating board-level conversation as he is at charming a room at a charity gala. If your event has a specific industry focus, this can be discussed during the consultation so Nico arrives fully prepared.

Can I request a specific style of engagement or set particular expectations? +

Absolutely. Every engagement is bespoke by design. During your consultation you are encouraged to outline the dynamic you require — whether that is a confident, assertive presence that commands attention, or a quietly supportive companion who operates in the background. Your comfort and objectives are the only brief that matters.
