What It Is

More Than a Date. A Partner Who Belongs.

The Husband Experience is Nico's most requested service for a reason. It was created for women who need a convincing, polished partner by their side for life's most important social occasions: family gatherings, weddings, Christmas parties, client dinners, and any event where arriving as a couple changes the dynamic entirely.

This is not a dinner date. This is a complete engagement. Nico prepares thoroughly for every event: understanding the relationships, the guest dynamics, the context of the occasion, and the role you need him to play. He arrives as your partner and performs that role with total authenticity from the first introduction to the final goodbye.

Every detail is handled. The way he holds himself at the table. The way he engages with your colleagues, your family, your friends. The way he reads the room and supports you without overshadowing you. It is a partnership that makes you look good, feel confident, and walk into the room knowing you have the right person beside you.

Questions

Everything You Need to Know

What is The Husband Experience exactly? +

The Husband Experience is a tailored companion service where Nico accompanies you as your partner for occasions where having a date makes all the difference. This includes family gatherings, weddings, work Christmas parties, holiday events, client dinners, and any social occasion where arriving with a polished, charming partner elevates the experience. Nico handles introductions, conversation, and social dynamics with complete authenticity, stepping naturally into the role so you can focus on enjoying the event.

How is this different from a regular companion booking? +

A regular companion booking is designed for events where you want a polished, engaging guest. The Husband Experience is specifically designed for occasions where a partner is expected rather than a date. This means more thorough preparation: understanding family relationships, guest dynamics, the context of the occasion, and the specific role you need Nico to play. It requires a higher level of adaptation and authenticity, and it is the most requested service for a reason.

Will anyone know it is not a real relationship? +

No. Nico presents as a naturally composed, socially fluent partner. He matches the context you establish: your preferred backstory, how you wish to be introduced, the nature of your relationship as presented to others. Every interaction is handled with the authenticity that has made Nico one of Australia's most sought-after companions. The arrangement reads as exactly what it presents to be: two people attending an occasion together.

How far in advance should I book? +

For standard family events and social occasions, a lead time of one to two weeks is usually sufficient. For weddings, multi-day events, or occasions requiring significant preparation, we recommend booking at least three to four weeks in advance. Last-minute bookings may be accommodated depending on availability. Sofia will confirm during the initial consultation.

Is The Husband Experience available outside Adelaide? +

Yes. Nico is available across Australia for the Husband Experience. He travels to Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth, Canberra, Hobart, and the Gold Coast on a regular basis. International bookings are also considered for the right occasion. Additional travel fees may apply depending on the location and duration of the engagement.

How is my privacy protected? +

Discretion is the foundation of every engagement. Your name, contact details, and the nature of the arrangement are never disclosed to any third party. All correspondence is confidential, invoices are issued through a discreet professional entity, and Nico maintains absolute professional silence regarding all clients at all times, without exception.
