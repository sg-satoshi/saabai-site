Understanding the Service

What is a male companion for hire? +

A male companion for hire is a professional who accompanies clients to social events, corporate functions, private dinners, galas, and travel engagements. The role is social and intellectual — providing polished, attentive company for occasions where presence, conversation, and discretion matter. It is entirely distinct from any form of adult service; every engagement is a professional social arrangement conducted to the highest standard of conduct.

Is hiring a male companion legal in Australia? +

Yes. Social companionship is entirely legal across all Australian states and territories. Nico Moretti's services are strictly professional social engagements — accompanying clients to events, providing intellectual company, and supporting clients at formal occasions. There is no ambiguity in the nature of these arrangements.

What exactly does a companionship engagement include? +

An engagement is a fully tailored social experience. Nico accompanies you to your chosen occasion — whether a corporate function, gala, or private dinner — as a polished, attentive, and intellectually engaging partner. All arrangements are handled with complete professionalism and discretion from first contact to conclusion.

What types of events do you attend? +

Nico attends a wide range of occasions, including:

  • Corporate gala dinners and board functions
  • Charity and philanthropic events
  • Private dinners and intimate gatherings
  • Arts and cultural evenings, theatre, and openings
  • Weddings and formal celebrations
  • Race days and exclusive sporting fixtures
  • Interstate and international travel
  • Personal occasions where refined company is preferred

If your occasion is not listed, please inquire — the answer is almost always yes.

What is the difference between a companion and an escort? +

In common usage the terms overlap, but the distinction that matters is in the nature of the service. Nico's practice is defined entirely by social and intellectual engagement — attending events, providing intelligent conversation, and representing clients with elegance at formal occasions. The focus is on substance: who you bring to the table says something about you, and Nico is chosen because he adds to an occasion rather than simply filling a role.

Privacy & Discretion

How is my privacy protected? +

Discretion is the foundation of every engagement. Your name, contact details, and the nature of the arrangement are never disclosed to any third party. All correspondence is confidential, invoices are issued discreetly, and Nico maintains absolute professional silence regarding any client at all times.

Will our arrangement be recognisable to others at the event? +

No. Nico presents as a naturally composed, socially fluent guest. He introduces himself according to whatever context you prefer and converses with ease across any room. The arrangement reads as exactly what it presents to be — two people attending an occasion together. Nothing more.

How is payment handled? +

Payment arrangements are discussed during the private consultation. Invoices are issued through a discreet professional entity and describe services in neutral terms. Multiple payment methods are accommodated to suit your preference and privacy requirements.

Booking & Logistics

How do I arrange an engagement? +

Begin by submitting an inquiry through the contact form. Nico's office will respond within 24 hours to arrange a brief private consultation — by phone or secure message — to discuss the occasion, your expectations, and any specific requirements. From there, all logistics are coordinated seamlessly on your behalf.

How far in advance should I book? +

A minimum of 72 hours notice is appreciated for most engagements, allowing time for proper preparation. For international travel, multi-day commitments, or events requiring a specific wardrobe brief, two weeks or more is preferred. Last-minute requests are considered on a case-by-case basis — do not hesitate to reach out.

Do you travel for engagements? +

Yes. Nico is available for domestic and international engagements. Travel arrangements, accommodation, and associated logistics are coordinated as part of the consultation process. There is no destination too far for the right occasion — Nico is as comfortable at a Milanese gala as a Sydney boardroom dinner.
